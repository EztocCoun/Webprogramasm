Frequently Asked Questions

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Please ensure that the product is in its original condition and packaging.

To create an account, click on the 'Register' button at the top of the homepage. Fill in the required fields in the form, including your email address, a password, and any other necessary information. After submitting the form, you'll receive a confirmation email. Follow the instructions in the email to complete your registration.

Yes, you can return or exchange items within 30 days of receipt. Items must be in their original condition and packaging. To initiate a return or exchange, please visit your account dashboard and select the order that contains the items you wish to return or exchange.

We accept a variety of payment methods including credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and online bank transfers. Specific options may vary depending on your location.

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email that includes a tracking number. You can use this number on our website's tracking page to check the status of your delivery at any time.

You can enter your promo code at checkout. There will be a box labeled "Promo Code" where you can enter the code before finalizing your purchase. Be sure to apply the promo code before completing your payment to benefit from the promotion.

If you've forgotten your password, click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.

You can update your shipping or billing address by going to your account settings. Click on 'Addresses' and you will be able to edit or add new addresses.

Yes, we offer international shipping. Shipping costs and times vary depending on the destination. Please refer to our shipping policy for detailed information.

Our customer service team can be reached via email at support@youandme.com, by phone at 1-800-123-4567, or through our online chat service. Our team is available 24/7 to help with any questions or concerns you may have.
